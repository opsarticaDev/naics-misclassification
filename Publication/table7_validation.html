Note: Population-weighted accuracy weights each stratum's reported accuracy by its share of the total population (394,234 records). Pipeline precision and recall treat all flagged strata (FLAGGED_NOT_PROMOTED, AUTO_RESOLVED, MANUAL_REVIEW) as "flagged" and CLEAN as "not flagged." False positives (FP) are records the pipeline flagged but that the reviewer determined were correctly coded. False negatives (FN) are CLEAN records that the reviewer found to be misclassified. Low precision is by design: the pipeline is a screening tool that catches 94.9% of misclassifications, with 97.3% of flagged records auto-resolved without human intervention. Suggestion accuracy reflects how often the pipeline's proposed alternative NAICS code matched the reviewer's independent determination.
